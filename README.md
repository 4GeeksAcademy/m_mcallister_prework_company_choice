# m_mcallister_prework_company_choice
For my company to work on, I chose TrackFlow. This company interests me because of it's real world application. All businesses have a shipping element and learning how to navigate that side of the business world, I hope, will help me with future career endeavours. 
Two departments whose problems I find the most interesting are their Customer Experience and Excutive Direction. The issues with their customer experience seem to be the easiest to fix, but would provide the biggest growth. Having a simple AI chat bot to fix any user issues would create a trust with their customers, which hopefully means they would be returning customers to their website. The issues with the executive direction are interesting because the reports the director are expecting need to have accurate and readily available data. However, with their manual reports that are made by the employees, this could lead to inaccurate data along with a slow turn around with any new data that comes in.
